# run0712 Job Plan

## Dataset
- Source dataset: `yen-0/so101-write-5-kadokawa`
- Blue-world derived dataset: `yen-0/so101-write-5-kadokawa-blue-world`

## Submitted Jobs
1. Target-image SmolVLA training on the source dataset.
2. Blue-world dataset filtering and Blue-world SmolVLA training in one PBS job.

## Inference Wrappers
- `eval.sh` loads the target-image policy bundle and uses `TARGET_DRAWING_PATH` at runtime.
- `eval_blue.sh` loads the blue-world policy bundle and uses `TARGET_DRAWING_PATH` plus the saved blue-world filter in the policy processor bundle.

## Policy Repos
- Target-image policy: `yen-0/smolvla-so101-digits-0707`
- Blue-world policy: `yen-0/smolvla-so101-digits-0707-blue-world`
